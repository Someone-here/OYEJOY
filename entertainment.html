Top 5 Most Underrated Indian Web Series On Youtube


Today there is so much content available on the internet that sometimes it gets hard to figure the best content to watch. With the increasing demand of content in the form of short videos, films, Web shows, web series they get uploaded almost every single day.
But this also leads to degradation and compromise with the quality of the content and hence in order for you to watch the best of the free to access content,
Here’s the list that contains top five most underrated indian web series that you can binge watch for free on youtube right now.

1. What the Goat (2017)


Genre = Drama, comedy
Episodes = 4
Running time = 1.5 hr
What The Goat, is one of the best web series based on entrepreneurship which revolves around the journey of three friends who come from totally different walks of life and go to Bangalore with the dream of fulfilling their Startup idea.
Their journey encapsulates failures, rejections and a lot of drama with an essence of humor. The best thing about this web series is that it isn’t just another startup web series and doesn’t simply glorify the perks of entrepreneurship but rather shows the real struggles, highs-lows and reality that an aspiring entrepreneur has to go through.
Watching this web series that is divided into four episodes is not just entertaining but also inspirational and motivational at the same time.

2. Friends.reunion.goa(2018)


Genre= Drama
Episodes= 5
Running time= 2h30m
Channel= Cheers
How would it be to meet your college friends after five years or so when each of you will be on different paths of your life. Can’t predict yours but watch the story of these four friends reuniting in goa 5 years after passing out from college, fulfilling their promise to meet each other.
Friends.Reunion.Goa is another underrated web series that tells the tale of friends, memories and experiences.

3. Office politics(2019)


Genre= Drama
Episodes= 4
Running time= 55m
Channel=GABBLIN
Office politics as the name suggests is about the politics that go around in the corporate sector of India. The story revolves around an ideal employee in a consultant company who denies to follow the sycophant atmosphere of corporate and gets victimized and struggles inspite of being hardworking and sincere.

4. Life sahi hai(2016)

Genre= comedy-drama
Episodes= 5
Running time= 1h15m
Channel= The lazy studio
This web series is of totally different genre and one can also call it Bollywood style as it has been made by the same people who gave Pyaar ka Punchnama.
This is the story that revolves around four flatmates who have their own struggles but more than anything, entertainment part of this story has made it to come into this list. Even the description of the show itself is quite amusing and goes something like this- What do you do when- daaru khattam ho gayi, cigarette bhi kam ho gayi, beedi ka katta reh gaya, girlfriend bhag gayi, chidiya sar pe hug gayi, naukari ne zindagi mein daala hai daaka? Well, you say “Life Sahi Hai”!

5. Baked(2016)


Genre= comedy-drama
Episodes= 7
Running time= 3h
Channel= scoopwhoop
Baked is the kind of web series that has not been underrated but may have been missed by a few people as it came at the times when concept of web series have just started to emerge in india. The story revolves around the misadventures of three university flatmates while their midnight food delivery startup and university life.
