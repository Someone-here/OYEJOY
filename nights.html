How does crowdfunded night work?

We first do a small survey among people and collects information on their interests. Based on that survey, we further plan and organize the event for the crowd.

Transparency

We highly believe in transparency and hence offer full transparency of the night budget and events and also share the details with every registrant.

Further details

Further details related to expenditure, discount offers, expense per head are shared only once the registrations are done and are shared only with the registrants of Crowdfunded Night.
